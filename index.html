About Us

Loftti Cafe is a small business that opened in August of 2018. Serving delicious juice, coffee, teas, boba tea, smoothies, small eats and desserts.
We are located in the lovely Southwest of Las Vegas, Clark County serving you day and night.
Surrounded by a hip and modern atmosphere with warm tones to bring you comfort. Stop by to socialize or to do some homework while endulging in our new take on teas and desserts or traditional classics.
